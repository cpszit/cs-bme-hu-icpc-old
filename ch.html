Important News (April 4, 2003)

Have you ever felt that you were stuck with a problem and there was no way out? Or have you ever heard the cock crowing while you were still adding some 'minor' changes to your work before the deadline? Here is your chance to measure your capabilities, to compare yourselves to other programming teams! All you have to do to compete for the prize is to form a team and enter the contest!

The Electrical Engineering Students' Hungarian Association and the Computing Sciences Group in Schönherz Zoltán Dormitory are proud to announce the 3rd BUTE International 24-hour Programming Contest held from the 16th to the 18th of May 2003.

Application is accepted by groups of three consisting of participants from any country. In the first, qualifying, round of the contest teams have to solve problems announced on the Internet. The best 40 teams will be invited to Budapest, Hungary, where they will have to complete a single but very complex task. Participants use their own computers without any restrictions to operating system, development tools or programming platform.

The total amount of the awards is 4.000 EUR.

You might want to check out the webpage of the previous year's contest and the photo gallery of the event.
